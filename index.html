Frequently
asked questions.
How do artists submit their music?
+
You publish a branded pitch portal URL. Artists visit it and upload their tracks, bio, social links, and EPK — no account required. All submissions land directly in your Pitchhood inbox, structured and ready to review.
Can I invite my team to review pitches together?
+
Yes. You can invite unlimited collaborators (on Label plan) who each score independently. Pitchhood aggregates scores, shows individual ratings, and highlights consensus — all in real time via Supabase.
Is it built with Next.js and Supabase?
+
Yes. Pitchhood is built with Next.js (App Router), Supabase for auth and database, deployed on Vercel, and version-controlled on GitHub via rocket.new. The stack is modern, scalable, and production-ready from day one.
Do artists get notified about their submission status?
+
Automatically. When you move an artist to a new stage in the pipeline, they receive a branded email notification. You can customize the messaging per stage — no manual emails needed.
Can I use it while I'm working elsewhere?
+
Absolutely. Pitchhood is a web app — accessible on any device, any time. Your team can review pitches async, and the Kanban board updates in real time so everyone stays aligned.
